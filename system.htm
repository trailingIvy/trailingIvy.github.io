System of Society
Different regions may often pride themselves on having their own particular and distinct customs.
However, the modern world has effectively become a "global village".
Each region actually operates within a greater system of society that is world-wide.
This system of society is called Capitalism.
Capitalism is recognised everywhere by the existence of Wages and Prices.
---------------------------
The alternative, Socialism, won't use Money.
Socialism will be based upon Voluntary Labour with Free Access to goods & services for All.
Production will be arranged directly in line with needs, and controlled democratically by and in the interest of the whole community.
