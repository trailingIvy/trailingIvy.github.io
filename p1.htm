P1: Ownership & Enslavement
How are decisions about the operation of society made?
What governs the quantities & qualities of production, or the social programs & laws that exist?
Decisions are not made based upon the needs of all. Some are excluded. Often many. Those that can't pay, don't get. Food that could feed the starving is regularly destroyed, or not even grown. Houses lie empty, or aren't ever built, because the poor are not a market. Profit, not people, determines the why, what, when & where of production.
It's Production for Sale at a Profit : Can't Pay? - Go Away!
A relentless, un-stoppable competitive chase for profits is destroying the ecology of the world. No matter, it seems, given the lack of governmental response, that this may then wipe out the human race. Poor quality goods are produced, not because people want to make, or have, junk, but because there's more profit in it. Everyone can think of examples of how decision-making puts profits before needs.
The rich get the best, the rest get seconds, or worse...
The Owners of the ' means of living' are responsible to no-one but themselves. Their interests enslave the rest of us. Society dances to their tune. Governments act to maintain and maximise profits for the owners as a group. We'll all tighten our belts, once again, for 'the Nation'. Sometimes one owner or group of owners may go 'bust', but overall, as a 'Class' they are are always winning.
Their profits always come first, regardless of anyone's pain.
Centuries of hard work and technological advances have greatly increased our productive capabilities. Yet production itself always comes in fits and starts. It's 'all hands to the pump', then it's 'no work here, today'. All we ever get is 'Boom' - then 'Bust', then ? All too often, any hard-won gains are swiftly ghosted away. They'll call it 'making economies'. As ever, we'll be the 'sacrifice'...
Decisions are not made in the majority workers' interest.
No-one has ever, or will ever, enjoy security as a 'wage-slave'. Disaster is only ever a job-loss or shut-down or illness away. And the gap between the very rich and the rest of us widens ever faster, every day.
Decisions are made in the minority owners' interests.
