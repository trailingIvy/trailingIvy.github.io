State Ownership ("Nationalisation")
State ownership is where a government runs a part, or the whole, of the economy directly.
Wages are still paid to workers, and Rent, Interest and Profit are still taken from their labours.
The working class still produces un-earned income for, and at the behest of, a privileged minority.
This goes either to the original Owners or to those who control the party/state apparatus.
The working class still does not own or control.
