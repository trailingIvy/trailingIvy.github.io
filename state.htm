State Ownership ("Nationalisation")
State ownership is where a government Owns and runs a part, or the whole, of the economy directly. Wages are still paid to workers, and Rent, Interest and Profit are still taken from the un-paid part of thir labour. The working class still produces un-earned income for, and at the behest of, a privileged minority. This goes either to the original Owners or, as in the self-proclaimed "communist" states, those who control the party/state apparatus.
The working class still does not own or control.
