Common Ownership
This means that society as a whole will own the means and instruments of production and distribution.
There will be none of today's divisions between Companies and Nations competing for Profit.
Everyone will own the world's resources : alternatively, no-one will.
It also implies these means will be under the democratic control of the whole community.
Everyone will be empowered equally in the rights - and responsibilities - of this control.
Common ownership will mean Wages and other Prices will no longer need to exist.
People will take from the common-store according to their self-determined needs.
They will contribute labour and time voluntarily.
-------------------
Money will not be used or needed.
Common ownership is clearly not the same as state ownership or 'Nationalisation' as it is often called.
Governments often take some, or even all, the Nation's industry under their own direct control.
It still produces only for sale (hopefully at a profit) and still pays it's workers in wages.
-------------------
Nationalisation changes nothing.
