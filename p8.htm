P8: Socialism Now
The Socialist Party of Great Britain is part of a global World Socialist Movement that believes capitalism cannot meet the needs of the majority of the people in the world. It does not today, and it never can. In order to meet these needs capitalism must be replaced by socialism.
The world working-class - it's wage-slaved - must work consciously and politically for this end.
The Socialist Party of Great Britain does not support the idea of reforming capitalism and therefore does not work for reforms. There are plenty of other organizations that do and yet the problems remain. By relegating socialism to the future, it is relegated to never.
Only a party dedicated solely to socialism can promote socialism in any real, honest manner.
Among all the political parties in Great Britain, only the Socialist Party is dedicated to socialism as an immediate goal. It is this objective that makes the Socialist Party revolutionary - our dedication to peaceful, democratic and immediate change. The Socialist Party is, therefore, engaged in a war of ideas against all other parties.
They, no matter what they claim, are supporting Capitalism by not supporting Socialism now.
Only the conscious action of the working class can end the Wages System of Capitalism and establish the CommonWealth of Socialism. The Socialist Party works only to increase understanding of, and mobilize support for, this end.
We call now upon every worker to support these efforts in any and every way that they can.
